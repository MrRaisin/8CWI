Tokyo

Tokyo, Japan's busy capital, mixes the ultramodern and the traditional, from neo-lit skyscrapers for its towering gate and surrounding woods. There are many things to do in Tokyo such as visiting the Meiji Jingu Shrine, going on the Tokyo Metro, or watch Sumo wrestling. Or you could simply walk around the city that bzzes with life.
